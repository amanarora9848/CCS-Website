Events At a Glimpse


Mozilla WebMaker

Overnight hackathon inspired by Mozilla WebMaker Party - an event that supports free content on the internet. Teams were given 12 hours and they had to develop a functional website and a comic strip based on given topics.

Coding Buffet

This was a competitive coding-based event where questions was based upon algorithms, mathematics and data structures. The one on the top of the leaderboard at the end was the winner.

Line follower

The teams had to make a Arduino based model which follows a black line on the floor. The team who’s model completed the circuit in the minimum time won.

Code Reaction

Coding-based competition in which the competitors, in a team had to solve problems by writing codes and find out which further were to be used as inputs for the next question. Participants were judged by the number of questions solved and time taken. Winner declared had the most accurate answers and least time taken to solve problems.
