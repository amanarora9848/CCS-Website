About US

CCS is one of the most elite technical societies of Thapar Institute Of Engineering and Technology. We strive to encourage students to develop an analytical temperament in the field of technology and innovation, nurture creativity and talent and spread awareness about the ever evolving tech world. We act as a platform for students to showcase and improve their skills and in doing so, discover that their best strengths lie within their own self. Over the years, the society has had numerous fruitful collaborations with organisations like Google Developers Group, Women Techmakers, Mozilla Webmaker and PyData, to name a few.
